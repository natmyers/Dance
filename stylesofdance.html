Styles of Dance
There are many styles of dance, the top 5 most popular are:

Ballet

Jazz

Hip hop

Contemporary

Ballroom
 Ballet is the most classical style of dance and Hip Hop being the most modern and funky
Ballroom dance is preformed with high heels on and usually with a partner
Jazz on the other hand is preformed in tan/black jazz shoes that have a thin slip sole to do turns
Contemporary is very emotional and expressive type of dance with lots of technique
